# Wavify Mobile - Design System

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Mobile-first
- Tone: Sexy, Premium, Dark Mode, Minimalist but striking
- Palette: 
  - Background: Pitch dark (#000000) or very deep charcoal (#121212)
  - Surface/Cards: Glassmorphism (semi-transparent dark with background blur blur-md)
  - Accent/Interactive: Vibrant Spotify-style green (#1DB954) wrapped over sleek components.
  - Text: Clean white (#FFFFFF) for primary, soft gray (#B3B3B3) for secondary metadata.
- Typography: Inter (clean, geometric, very readable).
- Styles: Fully rounded corners (`rounded-full`) for buttons, `rounded-xl`/`rounded-3xl` for album covers, and subtle neon shadow glows.
